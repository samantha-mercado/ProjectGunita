Hello, download and print all worksheets here! All worksheets use A4 size paper, although any size will do just fine.

WORKSHEET FAQS
    1. 1. Download the worksheet to be printed. It will be downloaded as a PDF file.
    2. 2. Open the PDF file in a new tab.
    3. 3. Click the printer icon on the top right corner of the screen.
    4. 4. Choose your desired paper size. All worksheets are in A4, but any sized paper will do!
    5. 5. Choose your desired printing mode (black and white or color). All worksheets will print well either way!
    Project Gunita also encourages the use of recycled paper for printing our worksheets!
