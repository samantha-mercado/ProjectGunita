What is Project Gunita?
Project Gunita is an educational website featuring videos and worksheets about reading, writing, speaking, and listening that aims to improve the literacy skills of Filipino children, ages 5-7 years old.
What is "Gunita"?
The word “gunita” means memory, reminiscence, at pag-alala. We chose the name “Project Gunita” because one of our objectives is to help Filipino children better retain and recollect the lessons they learn in school or at home.
Project Gunita was also meant to be a learning aid for children who were raised during the COVID-19 pandemic, where they were only familiar with digital classrooms and learning lessons through Zoom, to help them transition to traditional learning methods while still having a familiar way of learning through supplemental video-based materials.
Our mission
Our mission is to assist young Filipino learners in honing their literacy skills by providing free and accessible supplementary educational materials and a platform for safe and child-friendly learning.
Our vision
Our vision is to become a reliable educational platform where educators can access quality supplemental educational material for learning literacy skills. We envision a future where everyone has equal opportunities to learn, discover, and grow.
